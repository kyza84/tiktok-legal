# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data this application ("Service") processes when you use TikTok authorization and posting features.

## 1. Data we process
- TikTok OAuth tokens (access token, refresh token, expiration metadata).
- Basic account identifiers returned by TikTok APIs.
- Video metadata required for publishing (title/caption/privacy settings).

## 2. Purpose of processing
We process data only to:
- authenticate your TikTok account,
- upload and publish your videos,
- maintain technical operation of the Service.

## 3. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with TikTok APIs as required to provide functionality.

## 4. Data retention
OAuth and related operational data are retained only as long as needed for service operation, unless deletion is requested sooner.

## 5. User rights
You may request deletion of your stored data and revoke TikTok access at any time.

## 6. Security
Reasonable technical measures are used to protect tokens and account-related data.

## 7. Children's data
The Service is not intended for children under applicable age thresholds.

## 8. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
